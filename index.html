About Me

Design. Define. Deliver.

I’m Shiv, a visual and graphic designer who turns ideas into bold, eye-catching visuals.

Design has always been more than work for me — it’s how I think, express, and solve problems. What started as simple sketches and poster edits evolved into a deep love for branding, layout design, digital content, and creative storytelling.

Over the year, I’ve partnered with startups, influencers, and established companies to craft branding, digital experiences, and content that resonate. Every pixel, color, and frame we create is backed by strategic thinking — because we know good design isn’t just pretty, it performs.
